Our Organization

What is DFAMPC?

DFAMPC is a Multi-Purpose type of Cooperative whose memberships are composed of DFA employees and its attached agencies regardless of the nature of their appointments.

It was registered with the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) on January 10, 1991, under Registration No. QC 258.

When Republic Act 6938 was amended into Republic Act 9520 (The Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008), DFAMPC’s registration was confirmed on January 21, 2010, per Confirmation No. 9520-16006608.

Our Vision

"An Empowered Cooperative for a Sustainable Future"

OUR MISSION

Financial

To foster economic empowerment and sustained development for members.

LEARNING AND GROWTH

To equip its members to become entrepreneurs and innovative to sustain the growth and development of the cooperative for community and nation-building.

Internal Process

To capacitate officers and staff to improve and strengthen the cooperative’s operation.

Customer Satisfaction

To provide excellent products and services to satisfy members and stakeholders' needs.

Social Responsibility

To promote CSR by extending assistance to communities.
